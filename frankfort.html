Frankfort

Kentucky's Capitol

Kentucky's Political Center

The small town of Frankfort is rich in history. As one of the smallest captial cities in the United States, you have the freedom to explore as much as you please. Tour the capitol building, museums, and local businesses. You will cross back and forth over the Kentucky River, which winds through Frankfort at least four times.

Frankfort is positioned on a ford in the Kentucky River, which enabled pioneers to cross over.

The Kentucky capital is an well established spot for field trips, where school kids can learn more about Kentucky's history. Also, the state is Republican traditionally, but the current governor, Andy Beshear, is a Democrat.
