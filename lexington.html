Lexington

Home of the UK Wildcats

Heart of the Bluegrass State

Welcome to Lexington, the second-largest city in Kentucky. In spite of this urbanization, there are many horse farms in the surrounding area. Therefore, Lexington is named the horse capital of the US, and thoroughbreds are chosen breed trained for significant horse races. Keeneland is the most well-known racing track in Lexington. The Unviersity of Kentucky is one of top schools in the state, as well as a major NCAA powerhouse.

Kentucky Men's basketball last won the NCAA championship in 2012.

The UK Wildcats are represented by their basketball teams and football team. Additionally, Lexington hosts the Bluegrass music festival and one of the biggest Pride festivals in the state.

Kentucky offers top-class tourist experiences to visitors from all around the world!
