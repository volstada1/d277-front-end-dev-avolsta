Louisville

Home of the U of L Cardinals

In the 'Ville

Welcome to the largest city in Kentucky! Not only that, but Louisville boasts many points of significance. It is the birthplace of Muhammad Ali, the headquarters of KFC, and the home of the Kentucky Derby, one of the most prestigious horse racing events in the US.

In 2025, it was announced that KFC's headquarters will move to Plano Texas.

Right on the border with Indiana, and separated by the Ohio River, Louisville is a burgeoning progressive city. It is one of the most vibrant artistic and cultural centers in the South, as well as a prestigious center of education. In addition to U of L, other well known schools include Bellarmine University, Spalding University, and IU Southeast. U of L is known for its Engineering speed school and med school.

Kentucky offers top-class tourist experiences to visitors from all around the world!
